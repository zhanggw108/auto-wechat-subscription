## Product Purpose

AI 论文与行业热点公众号内容雷达 is a personal content cockpit that turns public AI news, paper, and project signals into 5-10 daily topic candidates, selects one front-page paper analysis, and produces a reviewable WeChat draft package with sources, evidence, checklist, Markdown, HTML, and image prompts. Success means one daily publishing package can be reviewed and prepared within 10-20 minutes without automatic posting.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, kinetic, editorial. The interface should feel like a working radar desk for someone who has taste and judgment, not a generic SaaS dashboard or an autonomous content farm.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI-purple gradient dashboards, oversized marketing heroes, decorative glass cards everywhere, empty report-like AI prose, and any UI that hides source evidence behind generated summaries. The product must not imply automatic publishing or unchecked factual authority.

## Design Principles

1. Evidence before prose: every important generated claim needs a visible source trail or risk note.
2. Decision speed over feature breadth: the first screen should help Gary quickly decide what is worth writing today.
3. Human-in-the-loop by design: selection, review, rerun, copy, publish status, and rejection are explicit manual steps.
4. Radar energy, editor calm: discovery screens can feel vivid and kinetic; writing and review screens must be comfortable for sustained reading.
5. Local-first MVP: the product should run without paid external services, while keeping clean adapters for real LLM and image2 calls.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for contrast and keyboard focus. Support reduced motion. Do not encode critical topic status by color alone. Keep editor and source panels readable for long review sessions.
